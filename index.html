How to make money while doing work that you enjoy ?

1. Figure out what you enjoy doing most.

Try to answer these questions and list them down:

  • What are the tasks, that feels like play to you?
  • What do you do in your spare time, when no one is watching?
  • If money weren't concern, what would you do?
  • What you would pay others to do?
  • What do you obsess over?
  • What do you dream about?
Include all irrespective whether its tangible, digital, activities, places or apps.
